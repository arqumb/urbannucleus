Return & Exchange Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction with UA sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags

Policy Overview

At Urban Nucleus, we want you to be completely satisfied with your UA sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags purchase. Our return and exchange policy is designed to protect both you and us while ensuring fair resolution of any issues.

Important Notice

360° Unboxing Video Required: All exchanges require a complete 360° unboxing video showing the entire unboxing process. This is mandatory for processing any return or exchange request.

What Can Be Returned/Exchanged

Size Issues (Our Error)

  • Wrong size sent due to our mistake
  • Size mismatch from what was ordered
  • Manufacturing size defect

Damaged Products

  • Items damaged during shipping
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Quality issues not visible in photos

Wrong Item

  • Different product than ordered
  • Color/model mismatch
  • Brand/model confusion

Customer Size Error

  • Incorrect size provided by customer
  • Size doesn't fit as expected
  • Changed mind about size

Used/Worn Items

  • Items worn after delivery
  • Items used outdoors
  • Items with signs of wear

No Video Documentation

  • Missing 360° unboxing video
  • Incomplete video documentation
  • Video doesn't show full process

360° Unboxing Video Requirements

Video Must Include

  • Complete Unboxing: From opening the package to removing the shoes
  • 360° Product View: Show all angles of the sneakers
  • Clear Visibility: Good lighting and clear camera work
  • Original Packaging: Show original box, labels, and tags
  • Issue Documentation: Clearly show any problems or defects

Video Quality Standards

  • Minimum 30 seconds duration
  • Clear, stable footage
  • Good lighting conditions
  • Audio is optional but helpful
  • File size under 100MB

Video Submission

Send your unboxing video via WhatsApp or Instagram DM. We'll review it within 24 hours and respond with our decision.

Time Limits

1

Contact Within 48 Hours

Report any issues within 48 hours of delivery

2

Submit Video Within 72 Hours

Provide unboxing video within 72 hours of delivery

3

Return Within 7 Days

Ship the item back within 7 days of approval

Late Requests

Requests submitted after the time limits will not be processed. We cannot accept returns for items that have been worn or used.

Exchange Process

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Contact Us: Reach out via WhatsApp or Instagram within 48 hours
  2. Describe Issue: Explain the problem clearly
  3. Submit Video: Provide the required 360° unboxing video
  4. Review Process: We'll review your case within 24 hours
  5. Approval/Denial: We'll inform you of our decision
  6. Return Instructions: If approved, we'll provide return shipping details
  7. Ship Back: Return the item in original condition
  8. Replacement/Refund: Receive your replacement or refund

Return Shipping

If the return is due to our error (wrong size, damaged item, wrong product), we'll cover the return shipping costs. For customer errors, return shipping is the customer's responsibility.

Refund Policy

Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds processed to the original payment method
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after receiving returned item
  • Partial Refunds: May apply if item is not in original condition

Exchange Options

  • Size Exchange: Exchange for correct size (if available)
  • Product Exchange: Exchange for different product
  • Store Credit: Credit for future purchases
  • Full Refund: Complete refund to original payment method

Item Condition Requirements

Items Must Be:

  • In original, unworn condition
  • With all original tags and labels
  • In original packaging (if available)
  • Free from any damage caused by customer
  • Clean and odor-free

Items Cannot Be:

  • Worn or used in any way
  • Damaged by customer misuse
  • Modified or altered
  • Missing original packaging or tags
  • Returned without proper documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to make an unboxing video?

A: The 360° unboxing video helps us verify the condition of items upon delivery and prevents fraudulent return claims. It protects both you and us.

Q: What if I don't have the original box?

A: While we prefer original packaging, we understand that boxes may be damaged during shipping. The video should show the condition of the packaging you received.

Q: Can I exchange for a different size if I ordered the wrong size?

A: If you provided the wrong size information, we cannot offer an exchange. Please use our size guide to ensure correct sizing before ordering.

Q: How long does the exchange process take?

A: Once approved, the exchange process typically takes 7-10 business days from when we receive your returned item.
