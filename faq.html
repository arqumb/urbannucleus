Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Urban Nucleus, UA sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags

About Our Products

What are UA sneakers?

UA (Unauthorized Authentic) sneakers are 100% genuine shoes made with the same materials and techniques as retail versions. They are produced by the same manufacturers but sold through alternative channels. They are NOT fake or counterfeit products.

Are UA sneakers fake or counterfeit?

No, UA sneakers are NOT fake. They are authentic products made in the same factories with the same materials as retail versions. The only difference is they are distributed through non-official channels, making them "unauthorized" but still genuine.

What brands do you carry?

We carry UA products from major brands including:

  • Sneakers: Nike, Jordan, Adidas, Yeezy, New Balance, Puma, and more
  • Watches: Rolex, Omega, Cartier, Panerai, Hublot, Audemars Piguet, and more
  • Bags: Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Hermès, Gucci, Prada, Dior, and more

Our inventory changes regularly, so check our Instagram or contact us for current availability.

How do I know the quality is good?

Our UA sneakers are made with the same materials and construction methods as retail versions. We carefully select our suppliers and inspect each pair before shipping. You can also check our Instagram for customer reviews and photos.

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. DM us on Instagram or WhatsApp with the product post/screenshot
  2. Provide your EUR/UK size
  3. We'll confirm availability
  4. Choose your payment method (UPI prepaid or COD)
  5. Confirm your order and shipping details

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • UPI Payments: Full payment upfront with free delivery
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): ₹200 advance payment required, remaining amount paid on delivery

Why do I need to provide EUR/UK size?

EUR and UK sizes are more accurate for UA sneakers as they match the original manufacturing specifications. US sizes can vary between brands, so EUR/UK sizes ensure you get the correct fit. Check our size guide for conversion charts.

How do I know if an item is available?

After you send us the product details and your size, we'll check our inventory and confirm availability within a few hours. We'll let you know if the item is in stock and ready to ship.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times:

  • Standard Delivery: 2-7 days
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 days (₹150 extra charge)
  • Remote Areas: May take longer than standard timeline

Do you ship everywhere in India?

Yes, we ship to all major cities and towns across India, including metro cities, tier 2 cities, and remote areas. Delivery times may vary based on location.

Is shipping free?

Shipping is free for all prepaid (UPI) orders. For COD orders, there's a ₹200 advance payment required, and additional delivery charges may apply for remote areas or failed delivery attempts.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive:

  • SMS notification with tracking number

  • Delivery confirmation notification

Returns & Exchanges

Can I return or exchange my order?

Yes, but only under specific conditions:

  • Wrong size sent due to our error
  • Damaged products during shipping
  • Wrong item received
  • MANDATORY: You must provide a 360° unboxing video

Why do I need to make an unboxing video?

The 360° unboxing video is required to:

  • Verify the condition of items upon delivery
  • Document any issues or damages
  • Prevent fraudulent return claims
  • Protect both you and us in case of disputes

What if I ordered the wrong size?

If you provided the wrong size information, we cannot offer an exchange. Please use our size guide to ensure correct sizing before ordering. We are not responsible for size issues due to incorrect size selection by the customer.

How long do I have to request a return?

You must:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Submit the unboxing video within 72 hours
  • Return the item within 7 days of approval

Watches & Bags

Do you carry luxury watches?

Yes! We carry a wide selection of luxury watches including Rolex, Omega, Cartier, Panerai, Hublot, and more. All our watches are UA (Unauthorized Authentic) - genuine products made with the same materials and craftsmanship as retail versions.

What about designer bags?

Absolutely! We offer designer bags from top luxury brands including Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Hermès, Gucci, Prada, Dior, and more. Our bags are UA quality - authentic materials and construction at unbeatable prices.

How do I choose the right watch size?

Watch sizing depends on your wrist size and personal preference:

  • Small wrists (5.5-6.5"): 36-38mm case diameter
  • Medium wrists (6.5-7.5"): 39-41mm case diameter
  • Large wrists (7.5-8.5"): 42-44mm case diameter
  • Extra large wrists (8.5"+): 45mm+ case diameter

Check our size guide for detailed measurements and examples.

What bag sizes do you offer?

We offer bags in various sizes to suit different needs:

  • Mini/Small: Perfect for evening events and essentials
  • Medium: Ideal for daily use and work
  • Large: Great for travel and shopping
  • Extra Large: Perfect for weekend trips and travel

Check our size guide for specific dimensions and examples.

Do watches come with original boxes and papers?

Most of our watches come with high-quality boxes and documentation that match the retail experience. However, availability may vary by model. Please ask about specific items when placing your order.

Are the bags made with real leather?

Yes! Our designer bags are made with authentic materials including real leather, canvas, and hardware that matches the original specifications. The quality and craftsmanship are identical to retail versions.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my correct size?

To find your correct size:

  1. Measure your foot length and width
  2. Use our size conversion chart
  3. Consider the brand (some run small/large)
  4. Think about sock thickness
  5. When in doubt, contact us for help

Do UA sneakers fit the same as retail?

Yes, UA sneakers are made with the same sizing specifications as retail versions. They should fit exactly the same as the original products.

What if I'm between sizes?

If you're between sizes, we recommend going up half a size for comfort, especially if you plan to wear the sneakers for extended periods or with thicker socks.
