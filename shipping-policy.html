Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery for your UA sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags

Shipping Information

At Urban Nucleus, we understand the excitement of receiving your new UA sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags. We're committed to providing fast, secure, and reliable shipping to get your order to you as quickly as possible.

What We Ship

We specialize in Unauthorized Authentic (UA) sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags - 100% genuine products made with the same materials and techniques as retail versions, produced by the same manufacturers but sold through alternative channels.

Shipping Methods

Prepaid Orders

FREE DELIVERY

All prepaid orders include complimentary shipping

  • Standard delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Tracked shipping included
  • Insurance coverage included

Cash on Delivery (COD)

₹200 ADVANCE

₹200 advance payment required for COD orders

  • Standard delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Tracked shipping included
  • Remaining amount paid on delivery

Delivery Areas

Domestic Shipping (India)

We deliver to all major cities and towns across India including:

  • Metro Cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad
  • Tier 2 Cities: Jaipur, Lucknow, Kanpur, Nagpur, Indore, Bhopal, Coimbatore, Kochi
  • Remote Areas: We also deliver to smaller towns and rural areas (delivery time may be extended)

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon.

Processing & Delivery Times

Order Processing

  • Prepaid Orders: Processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation
  • COD Orders: Processed within 24 hours of advance payment confirmation
  • Weekend Orders: Processed on the next business day

Delivery Timeline

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days from dispatch
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days from dispatch
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days from dispatch

Packaging & Protection

Your UA sneakers are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Original shoe boxes (when available) or premium replacement boxes
  • Protective bubble wrap and tissue paper
  • Tamper-evident packaging
  • Urban Nucleus branded packaging materials
  • Insurance coverage for all shipments

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive:

  • SMS notification with tracking number
  • Email confirmation with tracking details
  • Real-time tracking updates
  • Delivery confirmation notification

Important Notes

Before Ordering

  • Please provide your correct EUR/UK size when placing your order
  • We'll confirm availability before processing your order
  • Double-check your shipping address before confirming
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the package

Delivery Terms

  • Delivery attempts will be made during business hours (9 AM - 7 PM)
  • If delivery fails, the package will be held at the local courier office for 3 days
  • Additional delivery charges may apply for re-delivery attempts
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect addresses or recipient unavailability
