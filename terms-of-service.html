Urban Nucleus specializes in Unauthorized Authentic (UA) sneakers, luxury watches, and designer bags. These are 100% genuine products made with the same materials and techniques as retail versions, produced by the same manufacturers but sold through alternative channels.

Our UA products are NOT fake or counterfeit. They are authentic products made in the same factories with the same materials as retail versions, but distributed through non-official channels.
